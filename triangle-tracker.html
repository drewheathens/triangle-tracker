TRIANGLE-TRACKER!

This should be an exiting experiense!

As a user, you will enter the 3 sides of a triangle. After entering all the 3 sides
of a triangle, you should get one of the following responses:

  1. Equilateral: All sides are equal.
  2. Isosceles: Exactly 2 sides are equal.
  3. Scalene: No sides are equal.
  4. NOT a triangle: The sum of the lengths of any two sides of a triangle
    is less than or equal to the length of the third side.

Most importantly if "NO" or other characters are entered, Not a number alert
is given.

If Ready to begin press the "start" button below.
